Run Calorie Calculator

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the calories burned during your run, incorporating several key factors:

  • Weight: Calories burned are directly proportional to the body weight of the runner. We use your weight to calculate the total energy expenditure, converting pounds to kilograms if necessary.
  • Distance: The longer the distance, the more calories burned. This input adjusts the energy cost proportionally to the distance covered.
  • Duration: Total time spent running also affects calorie burn. We break down the input into hours, minutes, and seconds to compute the energy expenditure accurately.
  • Elevation Gain: Running uphill increases the energy cost significantly due to the additional effort required to overcome gravity. Elevation is optional and can be specified in meters or feet.

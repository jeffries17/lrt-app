About Age Grading

This calculator adjusts your running performance to compare it to world standards for your age and gender. It helps you understand how your performance stacks up against others and can help you track your progress over time.

FAQ

How does the calculator work?

The calculator uses your age, gender, and race performance to determine an age-graded score. This score allows for comparisons across different ages and genders. It adjusts your performance based on world record data to provide a standardized metric.

Why is age grading important?

Age grading provides a standardized way to compare running performances across different ages and genders. It helps identify progress and areas for improvement, making it easier to set realistic goals and track your development over time.

What distances are supported?

The calculator supports a variety of distances including 1 Mile, 5K, 10K, Half Marathon, and Marathon, among others. This range allows runners to evaluate their performance across different race lengths.

How accurate is the age grading?

Age grading uses established formulas and world record data to provide a reliable comparison. However, individual performances can vary due to factors like course difficulty, weather, and personal fitness levels. Use the age-graded score as a guideline rather than an absolute measure.

Can I use this calculator for other activities?

This calculator is specifically designed for running. Different activities have different performance metrics, so the results may not be accurate for other activities. For accurate comparisons in other sports, use calculators tailored to those specific activities.

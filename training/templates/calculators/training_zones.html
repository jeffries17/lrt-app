About Heart Rate Training Zones

Heart rate training zones are critical for maximizing the efficiency of your training, tailored to your fitness goals such as weight loss or improving endurance. Each zone represents a different intensity level that affects how calories are burned and how your cardiovascular fitness improves.

Use this calculator to find your heart rate zones by entering your age, maximum, and resting heart rates.

FAQ

How does the calculator work?

The calculator determines your heart rate zones based on your maximum and resting heart rates. These zones help you tailor your training to meet your fitness goals.

What is the importance of heart rate training zones?

Heart rate training zones allow you to train at different intensities, helping you improve endurance, speed, and overall cardiovascular fitness.

How accurate are the results?

The results provide a general guideline based on common formulas for calculating heart rate zones. Individual variations may occur, and it's always best to consult with a healthcare professional.

What if I don't know my maximum heart rate?

If you don't know your maximum heart rate, the calculator uses the standard formula of 220 minus your age to estimate it.

How should I use my heart rate zones?

Use your heart rate zones to guide your training intensity. For example, Zone 1 is for recovery, while Zone 5 is for high-intensity efforts.

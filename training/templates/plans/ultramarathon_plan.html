Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about generating and using your ultramarathon training plan.

How do I choose the right training distance?

Select the race distance you are preparing for. If you are training for a non-standard distance, select "Custom" and enter the distance in the provided field.

How many weeks should I train?

We offer plans ranging from 16 to 22 weeks. Choose the duration that fits your schedule and training level. More weeks allow for a more gradual build-up and better adaptation.

What if I need to change the units?

You can choose between miles and kilometers in the "Unit" dropdown. The training plan will adjust accordingly.

What if I miss a week?

If you miss a week, don't try to make up all the missed workouts. Continue with your plan, and adjust the intensity if necessary. It's better to progress steadily than risk injury by overtraining.

Can I download or email my training plan?

Yes! After generating your plan, you can download it as a PDF or send it to your email using the provided buttons.

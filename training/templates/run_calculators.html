How to Use These Calculators

Our calculators are designed to help you optimize your training and race performance. Here's a brief guide on how to use them:

Order of Use

For the best results, we recommend using the calculators in the following order:

  • Start with the Training Paces Calculator to establish your training paces.
  • Use the Training Zones Calculator to understand your training intensity.
  • Plan your race day nutrition with the Running Snack Calculator and the Run Calorie Calculator.
  • Utilize the Race Improvement Calculator to see how your training intensity impacts your race performance.
  • Check your readiness with the Ready For Race Day calculator.
  • For longer races, use the Ultramarathon Pace Calculator.
  • Finally, see how age affects your performance with the Age Grade Calculator.
